# 🎭 Theatre and cinema — Vocabulary

| ------ | ------- | ------- | ---------------- | --------------- | ----- | -------- |
| production | noun | /prəˈdʌkʃən/ | vở diễn, bản dàn dựng | a particular staging of a play or show | We went to see a new **production** of Hamlet last night. | |

| performance | noun | /pəˈfɔːməns/ | màn trình diễn, sự thể hiện | the act of performing a play, music, etc. | Toby Cartwright gave a marvellous **performance**. | |
| rave review | noun phrase | /reɪv rɪˈvjuː/ | bài phê bình tán dương hết lời | a very enthusiastic written critique | It got **rave reviews** in the papers today. | |

| score | noun | /skɔː/ | nhạc nền (phim) | the music written for a film | The **score** is by Thomas Newman. | |
