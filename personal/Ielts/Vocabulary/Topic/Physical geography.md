# 🏔️ Physical geography — Vocabulary

| Từ gốc | Loại từ | Phát âm | Nghĩa tiếng Việt | Nghĩa tiếng Anh | Ví dụ | Hình ảnh |
| ------ | ------- | ------- | ---------------- | --------------- | ----- | -------- |
| coast | noun | /kəʊst/ | bờ biển (đường bờ) | the land along the edge of the sea | We drove along the **coast** for miles. | |
| shore | noun | /ʃɔː/ | bờ (biển, hồ) | the land at the edge of a sea, lake or large body of water | We walked along the **shore** at sunset. | |
| beach | noun | /biːtʃ/ | bãi biển | an area of sand or stones beside the sea | The **beach** was crowded with tourists. | |
| valley | noun | /ˈvæli/ | thung lũng | a low area of land between hills, often with a river | The river flows through a wide green **valley**. | |
| gorge | noun | /ɡɔːdʒ/ | hẻm núi (vách dốc đứng) | a valley with very steep sides | The river has cut a deep **gorge** into the rock. | |
| stream | noun | /striːm/ | suối, dòng nhỏ | a small river | A small **stream** ran past the cottage. | |
| foot | noun | /fʊt/ | chân núi | the lowest part or base of a mountain | We camped at the **foot** of the mountain. | |
| ridge | noun | /rɪdʒ/ | sống núi | a long narrow raised part of a mountain | We hiked along the **ridge** to the next peak. | |
| peak | noun | /piːk/ | đỉnh núi nhọn | the pointed top of a mountain | Snow covered the highest **peak**. | |
| summit | noun | /ˈsʌmɪt/ | đỉnh cao nhất | the highest point of a mountain | They finally reached the **summit** at dawn. | |
| glacier | noun | /ˈɡlæsiə/ | sông băng | a slow-moving river of ice | A huge **glacier** filled the upper valley. | |
| plain | noun | /pleɪn/ | đồng bằng | a large area of flat land where little grows | The landscape consists largely of barren **plains** and mountains. | |
| volcano | noun | /vɒlˈkeɪnəʊ/ | núi lửa | a mountain that can erupt with lava and gases | The island has active **volcanoes**. | |
| thermal spring | noun phrase | /ˈθɜːməl sprɪŋ/ | suối nước nóng | a spring of naturally hot water | Iceland is known for its **thermal springs** and geysers. | |
| geyser | noun | /ˈɡiːzə/ | mạch nước phun | a hole in the ground that sends out hot water and steam | The island is famous for its **geysers**. | |
| crops | noun | /krɒps/ | cây trồng, hoa màu | plants grown for food | Less than 1% of the land is suitable for growing **crops**. | |
| ice field | noun phrase | /aɪs fiːld/ | cánh đồng băng | a large area covered by ice | There are large **ice fields** in the south west. | |
