# 📱 Social media — Vocabulary

| Từ gốc | Loại từ | Phát âm | Nghĩa tiếng Việt | Nghĩa tiếng Anh | Ví dụ | Hình ảnh |
| ------ | ------- | ------- | ---------------- | --------------- | ----- | -------- |
| post | verb | /pəʊst/ | đăng bài | to put a message, photo or video online | My friend **posted** a video of her baby and it was shared over a thousand times. | |
| share | verb | /ʃeə/ | chia sẻ | to send or show content to other people online | My friend posted a video of her baby and it was **shared** over a thousand times. | |
| update your status | verb phrase | /ˈʌpdeɪt jɔː ˈsteɪtəs/ | cập nhật trạng thái | to post about what you are currently doing | I probably **update my status** about twice a week. | |
| friend request | noun phrase | /frend rɪˈkwest/ | lời mời kết bạn | an invitation to connect with someone on social media | Send me a **friend request**, and then you can see the pictures I took. | |
| subscribe | verb | /səbˈskraɪb/ | đăng ký theo dõi | to sign up to receive a channel's content | Over 100,000 people **subscribe** to his video channel. | |
| follow | verb | /ˈfɒləʊ/ | theo dõi | to choose to see someone's posts on social media | You can subscribe to his video channel and **follow** him on Twitter. | |
| social media app | noun phrase | /ˈsəʊʃl ˈmiːdiə æp/ | ứng dụng mạng xã hội | an application that lets users share content and connect | CatNapp is a new **social media app** that allows users to share information about their pets. | |
| profile | noun | /ˈprəʊfaɪl/ | hồ sơ cá nhân | a page with information about a user | You create a **profile** of your cat and upload a favourite photo. | |
| upload | verb | /ˌʌpˈləʊd/ | tải lên | to put a file onto the Internet | You create a profile of your cat, **upload** a favourite photo and include some basic information. | |
| update | noun | /ˈʌpdeɪt/ | bài cập nhật | a new piece of information posted online | You can then post **updates** about what your cat has been doing. | |
| link | noun | /lɪŋk/ | liên kết | a connection to another website | You can share **links** to things you find interesting. | |
| comment | verb | /ˈkɒment/ | bình luận | to write a response to a post | You can **comment** on other cats' profiles. | |
| rate | verb | /reɪt/ | đánh giá, xếp hạng | to give a score or judgement of quality | You can **rate** videos of other cats' adventures from one to five stars. | |
| view | noun | /vjuː/ | lượt xem | a time someone has watched a video | You can see how many **views** or likes your post got. | |
| like | noun | /laɪk/ | lượt thích | a time someone has clicked 'like' | My post got 24 **likes**. | |
| notification | noun | /ˌnəʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | thông báo | a message to tell you when something has happened | We found it annoying that you can't turn off **notifications** when a friend writes something new. | |
| create an account | verb phrase | /kriˈeɪt ən əˈkaʊnt/ | tạo tài khoản | to set up a new user profile on a site | Make sure your information is kept private when you **create an account**. | |
| private | adjective | /ˈpraɪvət/ | riêng tư | only seen by people you choose | If you have to give personal information, make sure that it's kept **private**. | |
| privacy settings | noun phrase | /ˈprɪvəsi ˈsetɪŋz/ | cài đặt quyền riêng tư | choices on your account about what people see on your profile | Always check your **privacy settings** regularly. | |
| profile picture | noun phrase | /ˈprəʊfaɪl ˈpɪktʃə/ | ảnh đại diện | the main photo that represents you on your profile | Often your **profile picture** is public, but you can make posts private. | |
| public | adjective | /ˈpʌblɪk/ | công khai | able to be seen by anyone | Often your profile picture is **public**, but you can make posts private. | |
| timeline | noun | /ˈtaɪmlaɪn/ | dòng thời gian | collection of photos and other posts on your profile | You can usually make posts on your **timeline** private. | |
| tag | verb | /tæɡ/ | gắn thẻ | to add someone's name and a link to their profile in a post | If you are **tagged** in someone else's post, it might be seen by a lot of people. | |
| direct message | noun phrase | /dəˈrekt ˈmesɪdʒ/ | tin nhắn riêng | messages that only the people in the conversation can see | Use **direct messages** for private conversations. | |
| account | noun | /əˈkaʊnt/ | tài khoản | a registered profile on a website or service | Delete old **accounts** on sites that you are not using anymore. | |
| default | noun | /dɪˈfɔːlt/ | mặc định | what happens automatically if you don't change it | The **default** privacy settings might change. | |
